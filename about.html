About Us

We are a group of four students that came together based on our love for chicken wings! Our search for wings has led us near and far and across all borders. We've done the research to bringing you the best wings to areas near you and we are excited to share our passion for wings with you and look forward for you to use Got Wings as your first and only stop for the Best Wings in town!


We are Anthony Nunez; Deborah Ridgard; Jairus Clark; and Yesenia Zurita. Yesenia Zurita and Deborah Ridgard on Front-End and User Interface. While Jairus Clark and Anthony Nunez worked on Back-End and User Experience.
